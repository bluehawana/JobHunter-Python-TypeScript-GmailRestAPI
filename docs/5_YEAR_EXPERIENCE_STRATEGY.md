# 5+ Year Experience Strategy - Technology Timeline Mapping

## The Problem

Recruiters rejected applications saying "you only have 1 year of .NET experience at Synteda" because they couldn't see .NET mentioned in other positions. We need to show **5+ years of continuous experience** with each technology across multiple positions.

## Solution: Technology Timeline Mapping

Show the SAME technology used across MULTIPLE positions spanning 5+ years, even if it was part of a broader role.

## Key Principles

1. **PRIMARY vs SECONDARY**: Mark which positions had the technology as primary focus vs secondary
2. **Be Truthful**: Only mention technologies you actually used, but mention them everywhere you used them
3. **Span 5+ Years**: Ensure the timeline shows 2019-2025 or 2020-2025 for each technology
4. **Include Education**: Mölndal Campus (Java Integration 2019-2021) counts as Java experience
5. **Include Projects**: Personal projects (AndroidAuto AI Bot) count as Android experience

## Verification

For each template, verify:
- ✅ Shows technology mentioned in at least 4-5 positions
- ✅ Timeline spans 5+ years (2019-2025 or 2020-2025)
- ✅ Each position has 3-5 bullets mentioning the technology
- ✅ Professional Summary mentions "5+ years of [technology]"
- ✅ Core Competencies section lists the technology prominently
